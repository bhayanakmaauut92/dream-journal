## 2. Multi-Account Strategy — Is It Worth It?

### The Math

| Approach | Accounts | Clips/day/account | Total clips | Views/day (at 1K avg) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Single account | 1 | 10 | 10 | 10,000 |
| 3 accounts | 3 | 5 | 15 | 15,000 |
| 5 accounts | 5 | 3 | 15 | 15,000 |
| 1 account (viral) | 1 | 10 (1 goes 50K+) | 10 | ~60,000 |

**Key insight:** One viral clip on one account (500K+ views) beats weeks of grinding on 5 accounts. The algorithm rewards *one* high-performing account more than it rewards multiple small ones.

### When Multi-Account Actually Works

| Scenario | Works? | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Same clip, different accounts, different captions | ✅ | Repurposes reach — like cross-posting |
| 5 accounts, original content each | ❌ | 5x effort, each starts from zero |
| 1 main + 3 niche repurpose accounts | ✅ | Main gets traction, niche accounts feed off it |
| 5 accounts all managed by VA | ⚠️ | Only if VA writes unique commentary for each |

### Recommendation

**Phase 1 (now):** Build ONE main AIMN account. Get 1M+ views on it first. The playbook is proven — TBPN did it with 1 account posting 2 videos/day.

**Phase 2 (after traction):** Add repurpose accounts. A VA takes your best-performing clip each day and posts it to 3-5 niche accounts with rewritten hooks/commentary specific to that audience (e.g., "AI for Marketers," "AI for Small Business," "AI for Devs").

**Phase 3 (at scale):** Run 5 accounts with Spikes Studio Pro ($59/mo) — all fed from the same recording session, each with different hook angles.

## 3. VA Workflow — What They'd Actually Do

### Job Description

| Task | Tool | Time/day |
|---|---|---|
| Upload raw recording to Opus Clip | Opus Clip | 2 min |
| Review 30 auto-generated clips, pick top 10 | Opus Clip | 15 min |
| Add AIMN brand overlay (if using HyperFrames) | HyperFrames or manual | 10 min |
| Write platform-specific captions | ChatGPT + human tweak | 10 min |
| Schedule across platforms via Repurpose.io | Repurpose.io | 5 min |
| Track which clips performed best | Native analytics | 5 min |

**Total:** ~45 min/day

### What They Need to Know
- Basic CapCut or Descript (for manual fixes)
- English proficiency for caption writing
- Brand voice: **"What does this AI news mean for YOUR business?"** — practical, not hype
- Tools: Opus Clip, Repurpose.io, Canva (for thumbnails)

### Where to Find
- Upwork / Freelancer: ~$300-500/mo for Indian/Southeast Asian VA
- OnlineJobs.ph: $400-600/mo for Filipino VA (better English, timezone-friendly)
- Specialized content agencies: $800-1200/mo (managed, trained)

## 6. The Real Bottleneck

It's not the tools. It's not the accounts. It's **hook quality.**

The difference between a clip getting 500 views vs 50,000 views is almost always the first 3 seconds. Tools can't fix a boring hook — they can only amplify what already works.

**Your comparative advantage:** The 3-host business runner format (vs solo talking heads) gives you natural chemistry, genuine disagreement, and relatable stories. That's the content gold. The tools just extract and distribute it.
